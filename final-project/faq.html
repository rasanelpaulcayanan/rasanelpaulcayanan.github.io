Frequently asked Questions:


Question: When is the best time to go rafting?

Answer: The best time to go is late March to October. It is when we think that the river is safest. If you are seeking a more action packed and thrilling time of year to go, then the spring months are the best. The spring is when the snowmelt is at its peak and the rivers have the most water in them. However, in the spring the minimum age is raised and those who want to raft this time of year must be a bit adventurous! The summertime is the best for families and those who are seeking a great way to cool off from the summer heat.

Question: Is rafting safe for kids?

Answer: Yes, rafting is safe for children too, but there are some exceptions of course. General rule is that people shouldn't bring babies on rafting tours, because even if it's the calmest river ever, babies are just too little to help themselves in any possible way.
